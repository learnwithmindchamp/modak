Story :

  • When I was smoller glowing up in Italy I remember all my good evenings eating cannelloni with my family. Cannelloni can be eaten every seasons and can always be special.


Ingredients :

  • 10 eggs
  • 100gr flour
  • Water
  • 200 gr tomato sauce

Instructions :

  1. Put 10 eggs
  2. And 100gr of flour
  3. Smash together
  4. Slowly add 2 cups of water
  5. Continue mixing until the dough is smooth and almost liquid.
  6. After that, put the 200gr of tomato sauce
  7. Lastly, take a pan and put it in the oven with the dough for 20 min
  8. Buon appetito!
